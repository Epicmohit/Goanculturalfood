Chicken Xacuti

Goan Chicken Xacuti is a rich, spicy, and aromatic curry from Goa, deeply influenced by Portuguese and Indian culinary traditions. Made with tender chicken cooked in a flavorful blend of roasted coconut, poppy seeds, dry red chilies, and whole spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves, it delivers a unique balance of heat, nuttiness, and warmth. The spices are dry-roasted and ground into a thick masala paste, giving the dish its signature deep color and bold flavor. Other Delicious Types of Goan Xacuti

Fish Xacuti

Less common but equally delicious - made with firm fish like kingfish (surmai) or pomfret. The spices are adjusted slightly to suit the delicate flavor of the fish

Egg Xacuti

A simpler, vegetarian-friendly version using boiled eggs in the same spiced coconut gravy. Quick and easy to prepare

Vegetable Xacuti

A mild, wholesome version made using vegetables like mushrooms, potatoes, peas, or mixed veggies - great for vegetarians or children.
